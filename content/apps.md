## Boostra Scroll To

<img src="/images/boostra-scroll-to-logo.png" alt="Boostra Scroll To" style="max-width: 200px; margin: 20px 0;">

### 100% FREE FOREVER

**The complete scroll button solution for your Shopify store**

Boostra Scroll To adds a floating scroll button that blends seamlessly into your theme. Let shoppers jump to top, bottom, or any page percentage in one click. Completely free, with all features unlocked.

---

### Core Features

**Smart Scroll Actions**
- Scroll to top
- Scroll to bottom
- Scroll to specific percentage (25%, 50%, 80%, etc.)

**Complete Design Customization**
- **Shape**: Pill, Rounded, or Square
- **Position**: Bottom right, Bottom left, Top right, Top left
- **Spacing**: Custom padding (vertical & horizontal)
- **Colors**: Background, text, icon, and hover states with alpha/transparency support
- **Content Options**: Icon only, Text only, or Icon + Text combined

**Icon Management**
- Built-in icon library (arrows, chevrons, carets, and more)
- Directional icons (up/down)
- **Custom Icons**: Upload your own SVG, PNG, JPG, or WebP via Shopify Files
- **SVG Recolor**: Toggle to apply your custom icon color or preserve original SVG colors

**Text Customization**
- Custom text content
- Font size control
- Bold and italic styles

**Intelligent Visibility Rules**
- **Display Control**: Always visible OR visible after scrolling threshold
- **Scroll Threshold**: Show after X% scrolled
- **Device Targeting**: All devices, Desktop only, or Mobile only
- **Page Targeting**:
  - All pages
  - Home page
  - Product pages
  - Collection pages
  - Blog pages & articles
  - Regular pages
  - Custom page selection

**Built-in Analytics Dashboard**
- **Key Metrics**: Total clicks, unique pages, peak hour
- **Hourly Activity**: Visual breakdown of usage patterns
- **Device Breakdown**: Desktop vs Mobile analytics
- **Top Pages**: See which pages get the most engagement
- **Time Ranges**: Today, Last 3 days, Last 7 days, Last 30 days
- Real-time refresh capability

**Live Preview**
- See your button design in real-time within the app admin
- All changes update instantly in the preview

**Guided Onboarding**
- Quick start in 3 easy steps
- Visual "how-to" guides
- One-click theme integration
- Built-in status checking and refresh

**Easy Integration**
- Theme App Extension / App Embed technology
- Compatible with all Online Store 2.0 themes
- No code required - activate directly in theme editor
- One-click enable/disable with status management

**Performance Optimized**
- Lightweight storefront script with no heavy dependencies
- Efficient click-based tracking (no scroll spam)
